In 2008, the Hudson River Environmental Conditions Observing System (HRECOS) was established to provide high frequency geographically distributed real-time data between Albany and New York Harbor. It builds upon existing monitoring and observing activities on the Hudson River estuary, including the Hudson River NERR System-Wide Monitoring Program (SWMP), the US Geological Survey, and the NYS DEC Rotating Integrated Basin Studies.
