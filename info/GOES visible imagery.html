The GOES (Geostationary Satellite) provides the commonly seen views of clouds from space. GOES data is routinely available twice an hour (:15 and :45 after the hour). During active weather situations, the satellites can be placed in rapid-scan mode, which provides more frequent updates. This layer is provided by Iowa Environmental Mesonet, Iowa State University Department of Agronomy.
