Welcome to the MARACOOS Assets Explorer. This site is designed to present near real-time observational assets and model forecasts that contribute to ocean monitoring in the MARACOOS domain. To maximize your user experience, here are a few key components of this site. Feel free to contact us with any questions or comments.
ASSETS
Click on a dot to view detailed observations in a popup window. Then click on a graph icon to view a graph of each observation (where available). If you see a magnifying glass in the Legends panel, you will find more observation dots the closer you zoom.
MODELS and OBSERVATIONS
Many of these layers allow you to customize their appearance. Click on the link below a legend to view your options.
You can click anywhere on the map to create a graph of a time-series extraction at your point of interest. Only one layer may be queried at a time, but you can adjust this by selecting a value from the Active model query layer list at any time.
