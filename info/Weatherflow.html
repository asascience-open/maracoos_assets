The WeatherFlow Coastal Mesonet: Starting with just a handful of stations in 1987, Weatherflow now owns and operates the WeatherFlow Coastal Mesonet - the largest professional mesoscale observing network (mesonet) in the United States - setting the standard in this rapidly growing industry. With planned expansion taking it over 400 stations in Spring 2009 and reporting in as frequently as every 5 minutes, this network provides an unparalleled ability to monitor real time wind and weather conditions along the Atlantic, Pacific, and Gulf of Mexico coastlines.
